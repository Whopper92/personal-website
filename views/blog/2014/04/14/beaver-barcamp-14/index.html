Beaver Barcamp 14!

As usual, Barcamp was quite a lot of fun for all of us Portland State students that made the trip down in the early hours of the morning. There were all kinds of interesting talks, some excellent Frisbee action, good food, and lots of space just to hang out in the Kelly Engineering Center.

Check out these photos from the event, taken from the offical Beaver Barcamp Facebook page!
