How to Contribute

A presentation for PuppetConf 2016 about contributing to Puppet open source projects.


This presentation was designed to help new contributors get up to speed on all of the necessary steps for contributing to Puppet open source projects. It was delivered at the PuppetConf 2016 Contributor Summit. The focus of the talk was technical, but also provided some general reassurance about the helpfulness of the community.

We pride ourselves on inclusivity at Puppet, and I greatly appreciated having the opportunity to co-present this topic to the audience.
