D3.js Talk

A presentation for Beaver Barcamp 2014 about data visualization on the web.


  • Demo Code on GitHub

This presentation is designed for those who are interested in the idea of data visualization but may not have much experience in web development. The talk is heavily based on Scott Murray's book Interactive Data Visualization for the Web, down to the general progression of topics.

Have a look at a slides to get a general idea of what the talk is all about!
