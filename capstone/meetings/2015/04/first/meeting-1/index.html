Team Meeting 1 - Welcome!

Agenda

  * Introductions & Information Exchange (15 mins)
    - Email, Phone numbers, Slack nicks, meeting availability (5 mins)
    - Experience and skills (5 mins)
    - Goals: what do we all hope to get our of this experience? (5 mins)

  * Team Resources (10 mins)
      - This website: http://willhopper.com/capstone
      - Team Slack: https://pdx-capstone-2015.slack.com
          * Get everybody on Slack (5 mins)
      - Scrum Board: https://pdx-capstone-2015.axosoft.com
          * Get everybody on Axosoft (5 mins)

  * Discussion: team rules (30 mins)
    - Volunteer for team scribe? (1 min)
    - Plan day / time / place for regular team meetings (5 mins)
    - Communication preferences and expectations (3 mins)

    - *** It is always okay not to know something! Ask questions!

    - Decision making process (3 mins)
      * Majority vote? Consensus?

    - Meeting rules (3 mins)
      * Responsibility for agendas and minutes
      * Attendance and participation expectations
      * Creation and announcement of meetings

    - Work balancing and team participation (15 mins)
      * Flexibility of team roles
      * Expectations on completing assigned work
      * Even distribution of work across team members
        - How do we want to assign work (tickets)?
        - Pairing
      * Ideas and opinions always welcome
        - Everyone is welcome to bring ideas / concerns / opinions to the team.

    - Conclusion

  * Any remaining time
    - Volunteer to research and setup a team wiki for documentation?
    - What sorts of projects might we want?
      * Project selection will be our first task after establishing a set of team rules

  * Future meetings
    - Project picking
    - Workshops
    - Technical walkthrough of scrum workflow
    - Discussion on Git workflow

Notes

  * Communication:
    - We’re cool with the Sunday Wednesday meeting times
    - We’ve decided Slack’s the default meeting tool
    - GIT: SourceTree and gitflow were mentioned as interfaces for using git
  * Project Selection:
    - We agreed we’d like to make sure everyone gets to talk and discuss about project
      selection, and general consensus that we don’t want to pick a project that someone
      seriously hates.
    - We discussed: Instant runoff? Or points based procedure for project selection,
    - Jason mentioned he’d like a more complex project so we could learn multiple different
      things and have plenty to work on in tandem
  * Work balancing and participation:
    - We generally agreed to ‘wear different hats’ over the course of the project and to
      share/pair on certain tickets.
    - We’re going to try to finish our individual tickets and everyone’s welcome to create
      and take extra tickets if they need to and have time. Generally, feature tickets
      will already be planned and exist, but bug tickets will need to be created as
      they're discovered.
  * Licenses to Check out:
    - GPL, LGPL, Apache, BSD, MIT, etc.

  * Action Items:
    - Will to setup GitHub repo/wiki
    - Team to research various software licenses
    - Max, Peter and Will to begin preparing for git workflow workshop
    - Jason and Peter to begin looking into Travis CI
